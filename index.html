Sushant Singh Rajput

        Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor-dancer who worked in Bollywood. He was born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar and he passed away on June 14, 2020, in Mumbai,Maharastra. He starred in number of commercially succesful Hindi films such as M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story, Kedarnath and Chhichhore.

    Sushant was good at studies and secured seventh rank in Delhi College of Engineering Entrance Exam. He was also a National Olympaid Winner in Physics. Sushant started his career after dropping out of his engineering course at Delhi College of Engineering and entering the theatre industry in Mumbai.
